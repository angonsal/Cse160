Notes to Grader

You probably wont see this on the html page, but I will write it anyways and copy and paste to canvas comments. This project is very fun! My goal is to create a quidditch scene from Harry Potter. I added over 20 shapes; and 4 different types of light sources. There is also a skybox which looks like a quidditch field, and orbital controls. I added a frog in addtion to the cat because those are two very popular pets at Hogwarts. I added two boxes of quidditch balls because in this scene they are playing quidditch. I added a table on both ends of the field; one with a trophy, and one with the sorcerer's stone.
